UFOs are real. But can we assume they're Alien Spaceships?

There are thousands and thousands of reported UFO sightings in the United States since 1900. Often Aliens is the default explanation for those weird stuff in the sky. We would love to know: Are we actually alone? But maybe we shouldn't jump to this E.T conclusion so quickly. We are right to be sceptic because if aliens where here they are very shy and probably the best houseguest ever. What could be a simple explanation? Planes, manifestations of drugs, alcohol, mental illness, military bases exercices, new year fireworks ? They are multiple explanations possible for those manifestations. By this website we are trying to unravel some of this mystery by looking through 2012 to 2014 UFO sightings.

About this study

  • This study is about UFO's reports done in USA between 2012 and 2014. In fact, the USA take UFO question very seriously and the National UFO research center (NUFORC) have been collected more than 100 000 reports since 1950. To led this study, our team made an analysis of needs, then clean the data and integrate it in a data warehouse. On this website you can find the reporting work.

  • Context

    In the USA, aliens are the default explanation for UFOs. There are a lot of examples of americans' fascination for plot theory involving aliens. Last one was about the Area 51, more than 2 millions people planed to assault this military base because they feared aliens were hiding there. That's why our team led this study, to know if UFOs can be aliens or just fireworks, military base exercises ...

  • Stack

    • SGBD : MySQL
    • ETL : Talend
    • Reporting : Power BI

  • Data base

    Our team use the NUFORC's data which contains all the UFO's reports with their date, hour, duration, location ... We complete these information with the binge alcohol use, the marijuana consumption and the mental illness in the country. We add US military bases location, united states codes and US holiday to refine our analysis.
